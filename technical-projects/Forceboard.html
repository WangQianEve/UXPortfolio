Forceboard
Pressure-based Text Entry

Forceboard is a interaction technique we created for typing that uses how hard you press an interface as the sole input. It's achieved through moving a sliding cursor to the desired letter with pressure and confirm with a quick release.

In the demo below, we used iphone's 3D touch technology to detect the pressure, and used Google glass to show the interface to the user.
